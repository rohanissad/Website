Our Product:

Our products are pieces of technology that we sell to governments and private companies. These agencies are able to use our machinery in order to clean our oceans, recycle this trash, and benefit our world. Our current product is a water based trash collector. It runs on Tesla solar panels and has a built in generator. It does not require a crew or anybody to be onboard however it is a remote controlled device. Its starting cost is 2,000,000 but can vary depending on company needs. Our protoype, or next project is focused on cleaning up our beaches. It sifts through the sand and collects the garbage in the back.
